### The Convex Question

Aki: "How was it able to integrate with convex? Did I not need a convex account or credentials or anything?"

Good question. I'd glossed over that.

**The answer:** Convex has two modes.

**Local mode** (what we're using):
- No account needed
- Runs on localhost:3210
- Data stored locally
- Perfect for development
- Not production-ready

**Cloud mode** (what we'd need for production):
- Requires Convex account
- Gets production URL (https://your-project.convex.cloud)
- Data in cloud, accessible remotely
- Costs money (but has generous free tier)

We're in local mode. Which is fine for now. But if we want to deploy the dashboard publicly, we'll need to move to cloud.

Explained this to Aki. He understood.
